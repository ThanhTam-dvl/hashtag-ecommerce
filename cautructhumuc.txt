## 🎨 **Theme Design System:**

### **Colors:**
- **Primary**: Blue (#3B82F6)
- **Background**: Pink/Rose tones
- **Accent**: Purple/Violet
- **Text**: Dark Gray
- **Success**: Green
- **Warning**: Orange
- **Error**: Red

### **Components Philosophy:**
- **Compact design** cho mobile
- **Card-based layout** 
- **Smooth animations**
- **Responsive grid system**
- **Touch-friendly buttons**

## 📋 **Quy tắc phát triển:**

### **Component Structure:**
1. **Customer components** trong `/customer/`
2. **Admin components** trong `/admin/` 
3. **Common components** dùng chung
4. **Page components** trong `/pages/`

### **Responsive Rules:**
- **Mobile-first approach**
- **Compact spacing** trên mobile
- **Grid optimization** cho phone/tablet/desktop
- **Touch targets** >= 44px

### **Code Organization:**
- **Feature-based** structure
- **Reusable components**
- **Consistent naming**
- **Props validation**
